Phony is a narrative based game where you take the role of a landlord and wander around a world and interact with the things around you. Interact with characters in silly scenarios and try your best to make the most of what you have. There is no dungeon boss, ranking system, or inevitable world apocalypse, but just about experiencing the the fleeting moments of life and learning more about the people around you and yourself.

Currently under pc development.
